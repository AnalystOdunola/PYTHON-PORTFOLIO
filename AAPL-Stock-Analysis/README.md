# AAPL Stock Performance Analysis (2021–2026)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Apple Inc. (AAPL) stock performance using historical Yahoo Finance data from June 2021 to June 2026.

The analysis was conducted using Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib to explore price trends, trading activity, returns, and market momentum through time-series analysis.

---

## Dataset

Source: Yahoo Finance

Period:
09 June 2021 – 09 June 2026

Variables:
- Date
- Open
- High
- Low
- Close
- Adjusted Close
- Volume

Approximately 1,370 trading records were analyzed.

### Trading Volume Analysis

### Daily Return Distribution

### Gain vs Loss Days

### Monthly Returns Analysis

Evaluates monthly stock performance trends.

---

## Key Insights

- AAPL increased from approximately $130 to over $310 between 2021 and 2026.
- Approximately 138% price growth was recorded.
- 53.3% of trading days were positive.
- Long-term momentum remained strong throughout the period.
- Monthly performance was predominantly positive.
